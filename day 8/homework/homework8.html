A non-fungible token (NFT) is unique digital identifier that is recorded on a blockchain and is used to certify ownership and authenticity. it cannot be copied,substituted , or subvidided.The ownershop of an NFT is recorded in the blockchain and can be transfered by the owner, allowing NFTs to be sold and traded.NFTs can be created by anybody and require few or no coding skills to create. NFTs typically contain reference to digital files such as artworks,photos,videos, and audio. because NFTs are uniquely identifiable, they differ from cryptocurrencies,which are fungible.
